Bolts and Nuts

Bolts and Nuts is an incredibly fun puzzle game. It requires you to use logic and strategy to solve unique puzzles.

Your goal is to remove the bolts so that the wooden boards they are holding fall off the screen. Beat 100 levels of Bolts and Nuts to become the best!

How to play Bolts and Nuts

Remove the bolt: Select the bolt you want to remove and click on it. Once removed, you need to place the bolt in an empty hole.

Rearrange the bolts: Moving the bolts to different empty holes is part of the strategy. Look for bolts that can be removed without covering any empty holes.

Support tools

  • Screwdriver: Use to remove the bolt you click on.
  • Freeze time: Stop the countdown timer for 30 seconds, giving you more time to complete the level.

Tips and Tricks

You can unlock bolt holes by watching short ads.

Use bonus points to get more bolt removal turns when needed.

Have fun and relax playing Bolts and Nuts!
