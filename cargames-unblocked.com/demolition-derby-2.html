Demolition Derby 2

What is Demolition Derby 2?

Demolition Derby 2 is an action-packed racing game where players control vehicles in an arena and compete to cause the most damage to opponents' cars. The goal is to survive crashes and collisions while trying to eliminate other drivers. It offers various game modes and customization options for vehicles, making it a thrilling experience for fans of destructive racing games.

How to play Demolition Derby 2?

In Demolition Derby 2, players control a car in an arena and aim to crash into other vehicles to cause damage and reduce their opponents' health. The game offers different modes, including survival and timed events. Players earn money for winning and causing damage, which can be used to upgrade and customize their cars. The objective is to be the last driver standing or achieve the highest score in the given game mode.
